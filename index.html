  • Only Shiny: Bot will only catch the shiny version.
4. Archetypes: The Orange Button adds a Trio: Bulk (Rank 1), Mid (+5% Atk), and Slayer (+10% Atk with survival logic).
5. Download: Add your Pokémon, then hit the green download button and import to Polygon.
